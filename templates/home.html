About Bughead

Bughead was born from a simple idea: make bug reporting accessible to everyone, not just developers. We believe that by bridging the gap between users and developers, software quality improves dramatically.

Our platform simplifies the bug reporting process, enhances developer feedback loops, and helps build cleaner, better digital products that users love.
